Toxin Storage and disposal

Toxins are bought in their solid form, and can be safely stored under room temperature. They will be stored in a specific cabinet for dangerous chemicals in the lab.

Any waste that had come in contact with the toxins during experiments must be disposed into specific medical waste bins, clearly labelled "Lethal Toxin".

We have designed the following warning sticker to stick onto the outer-surface of the bottle containing the toxin, and any containers or trash cans designated specifically to toxic waste disposal:

Aside from this, any places for temporary storage of the toxic waste will be circled out using a cordon, to make sure that no unrelated people come in close contact with the toxin-containing waste.

Detoxification of experimental instruments, flasks and test tubes

Due to the oxidative reactivity of potassium manganate(VII), commonly known as potassium permanganate (KMnO4), it can effectively oxidize and detoxify the amatoxins. We prepare a pool of potassium manganate(VII) aqueous solution of concentration 20g/L. Any instrument must be soaked in the solution for at least 5 minutes before they can be thoroughly rinsed with water.

Alternatively, instruments can be soaked in concentrated sodium hydroxide (NaOH) aqueous solution for 5 minutes to remove the toxicity of amanitins.

During Experimentations

The following precautions must be made before conducting experiments involving the toxins:

  • Wear closed-toe shoes
  • Wear long pants
  • Wear formal lab coats
  • Long hair must be tied up
  • Wear gloves
  • Wear goggles
  • Wear facemasks
  • Make sure no skin is exposed to air
  • Absolutely no food or drink in lab areas
  • Make sure experiments are supervised at all times
  • Make sure all containers are closely sealed and will not leak

And the following must be done after experiments:

  • Dispose one-time gloves and facemasks into designated trash bins. Make sure they do not come in contact with your skin while taking off
  • Wash hands and face thoroughly, for at least 5 minutes
  • Change clothes after experiments
